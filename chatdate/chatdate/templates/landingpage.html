SwoonLounge is an online community for meeting and chatting with other people in your area. Unlike other online dating sites where window shopping is the name of the game, SwoonLounge encourages interaction over compatibility matching algorithms. Currently SwoonLounge is in beta, so there aren't very many people to chat with on the site.

How it works

As you chat with other people, your karma score increases. People with higher karma gain more trust among other users of the site, and get to interact with more people.
